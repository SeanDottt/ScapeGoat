SCAPEGOAT PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

At Scapegoat, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the operational and personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how Scapegoat ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information gathered through our proprietary front-end conversion tools and dynamic intake forms embedded across our network of partner websites.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To generate accurate, real-time dynamic fulfillment rates, we collect specific metrics when you interact with our platform:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, and any other contact details submitted through the Services.
  • Business and Logistics Profile Information: Inbound and outbound shipment volumes, average pallet storage counts, square footage requirements, product category, storage type, handling requirements, order volume, SKU profile, shipping profile, and other fulfillment-related information submitted through the Services.
  • Technical and Usage Information: We may collect limited technical information associated with form submissions, such as IP address, device information, browser information, submission timestamp, referring website, and interaction data, where reasonably necessary for security, recordkeeping, analytics, troubleshooting, and verification of form submissions.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to operate the Scapegoat routing and quoting engine and to support fulfillment-related business introductions. Specifically, your information may be used to:

  • Calculate automated, dynamic fulfillment pricing estimates.
  • Generate customized quote summaries, including on-screen quotes, automated PDF summaries, and email introductions.
  • Evaluate whether the warehouse provider hosting the form may be able to support your fulfillment requirements.
  • Match your submitted fulfillment requirements with one or more logistics providers whose services, infrastructure, location, or capacity may align with your logistics profile.
  • Communicate with you regarding fulfillment services related to your request.
  • Maintain records of form submissions, improve the Services, troubleshoot technical issues, protect against misuse, and support platform security.

3. NETWORK ROUTING AND DATA DISCLOSURE FOR QUOTE EVALUATION

Scapegoat does not sell submitted information to data brokers or third-party advertising companies and does not use submitted logistics profile information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Scapegoat operates a logistics quote-routing model that may disclose submitted business contact information and logistics profile information to the warehouse provider hosting the form and to matched logistics providers for the purpose of evaluating fulfillment fit, generating estimated pricing, and following up regarding fulfillment services requested by you.

By submitting an intake form through the Services, you authorize Scapegoat, the warehouse provider hosting the form, and matched logistics providers within the Scapegoat network to use and disclose your submitted business contact information and logistics profile information for the limited purposes described in this Policy, including quote evaluation, business introduction, and related follow-up regarding fulfillment services requested by you.

  • Initial Evaluation: Your submitted information may be reviewed by the warehouse provider whose website hosts the Scapegoat-powered form, referred to as the “Host 3PL,” to evaluate whether the Host 3PL may be able to support your fulfillment requirements.
  • Network Routing: If your submitted logistics profile indicates that another provider may be a better operational fit based on location, capacity, storage type, product profile, order volume, or other fulfillment requirements, Scapegoat may route your submitted business contact information and logistics profile information to one or more matched independent logistics providers within the Scapegoat network.
  • Alternative Quote Generation: Matched logistics providers may receive your submitted business contact information and logistics profile information for the limited purpose of evaluating your fulfillment requirements, generating estimated pricing, and contacting you regarding fulfillment services related to your request.

4. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

If you are a California resident, you may have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, with respect to personal information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked to you.

  • Right to Know and Access: You may have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources from which the information was collected, the purposes for which we collect, use, or disclose the information, and the categories of third parties to whom we disclose personal information.
  • Right to Delete: You may have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to applicable legal exceptions, operational requirements, and recordkeeping obligations.
  • Right to Correct: You may have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal information maintained about you.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing, If Applicable: Scapegoat does not use submitted logistics profile information for cross-context behavioral advertising. If Scapegoat determines that any disclosure of personal information constitutes a “sale” or “sharing” under applicable California privacy law, Scapegoat will provide the required notice and opt-out mechanism.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy rights available to you under applicable law.
  • Notice Regarding Quote Routing: By submitting an intake form through the Services, you authorize Scapegoat, the Host 3PL, and matched logistics providers to use and disclose your submitted business contact information and logistics profile information for the purpose of evaluating your fulfillment requirements, generating estimated pricing, facilitating business introductions, and contacting you regarding fulfillment services related to your request. Scapegoat does not use submitted logistics profile information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Exercising Your Rights: To submit a privacy request, including a request to access, delete, or correct personal information, you may contact us at rates@hawkishllc.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

5. NOTICE AT COLLECTION

At or before the time you submit information through the Services, Scapegoat may collect the categories of personal information described in this Policy, including contact information, business and logistics profile information, and limited technical and usage information.

We collect and use this information for the purposes of evaluating fulfillment requirements, calculating estimated pricing, generating quote summaries, facilitating business introductions, routing inquiries to the Host 3PL or matched logistics providers, communicating with you regarding fulfillment services, maintaining records of form submissions, improving the Services, and protecting the security and integrity of the platform.

Scapegoat does not use submitted logistics profile information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Scapegoat does not sell submitted information to data brokers or third-party advertising companies. Scapegoat may disclose submitted information to the Host 3PL and matched logistics providers for quote evaluation, business introduction, and related follow-up regarding fulfillment services requested by you.

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain business records, enforce agreements, and support security and operational requirements.

6. SECURITY OF YOUR DATA

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational security measures designed to protect submitted information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse. Operational metrics are transferred using commercially reasonable security measures and are intended to be accessible only to Scapegoat, the Host 3PL, matched logistics providers, and authorized service providers or personnel who need access for quote evaluation, business introduction, platform operation, security, or related follow-up purposes.

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Services, legal requirements, technology, or business practices. Any updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date at the top of this Policy. If we make material changes to how we collect, use, or disclose personal information, we will provide notice as required by applicable law.
